Paris is often referred to as the capital of gastronomy, and the title is richly deserved. The city is a dream destination for foodies and the ideal place to try authentic French Cuisine.Parisian cuisine is among the most exciting and varied in the world,and the capital's restaurants reflect this with innovative and originalmenus.Parisian food culture is characterized by its texture, taste, and type ofood. The city is home to some of the world's most talented chefs, pâtissiers,and chocolatiers. Its delis and food stores are second to none, and its famous cookery schools are recognized across the globe.


Parisian is not all about snails and frog legs. The city's restaurants offer a wide range of dishes, from the classic French onion soup to the famous croissants, macarons, baguettes, and croque-monsieurs. The baguette is the French specialty par excellence, characterized by its crusty exterior and soft interior. Cheeses from the Paris region are also a must-try, with the most well-known being the bries: from Meaux, Montereau, Melun, or Provins. Paris is also famous for its patisseries, with the Opéra cake being a popular choice. Freshly baked bread,cheese, onion soup, escargots, and macarons are just a few of the many foods to try in Paris.
